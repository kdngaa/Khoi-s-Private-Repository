My curiosity for programming began when I first discovered microblogs at the age of 13. Being able to create and customize my own blog through HTML and CSS was an oddly satisfying feeling as a child. This excitement soon came back to me when I started working in the tech solutions industry, amongst some of the most talented engineers and solutions architects. As a former B2B/B2C Product Marketer turned Software Engineer, my ability to communicate technological processes further advances my expertise in forging a viable product through intricate frameworks. In addition, my efficiency in learning and retaining information quickly enables me to become an asset in a fast-paced industry.

When I'm not busy building full-stack applications or debugging, you can find me collecting vintage t-shirts, reconstructing my 5-star Animal Crossing island, and spending time at natural history museums. I also enjoy staying up-to-date with the NBA, eating and trying new food, especially Southeast Asian street food. If you scroll down below, you can see that I often reference my hobbies into several of my projects. I am open to positions in both Southern and Northern California, as well as in any big city on the East Coast. Please feel free to connect or reach out to me via Gmail and LinkedIn!
